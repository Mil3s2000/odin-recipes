Tuwo with Beans Soup

Description

Tuwo: White, cooked, paste, soft.

Beans Soup: Yellow, thick.

Ingredients

  • Corn Flour
  • Grinded beans
  • Seasonings e.g Curry, Thyme, Maggi etc.
  • Crayfish
  • Red oil

Steps in making Tuwo

  1. Add 2 cups of water to pot and wait till it boils.
  2. Add some potash.
  3. Mix the amount of corn flower to be used with moderate amount of water.
  4. Pour the mixture of the corn flour and water into the boiling water.
  5. Stir the mixture together.
  6. Keep stiring until it becomes thick.

Steps in making Beans Soup

  1. Pour 2 cans of thinned beans into pot.
  2. Add a moderate amount of water.
  3. Add the seasonings and necessary ingredients of your liking.
  4. Bring the soup to simmer and cook for about 40 mins.
