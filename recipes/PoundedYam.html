Pounded-yam and Egusi Soup

Description

Pounded yam: White, solid, fluffy.

Egusi: Thick, yellow.

Ingredients

  • Tubers of Yam.
  • White seed melon.
  • Seasonings e.g Curry, Thyme, Maggi etc.
  • Crayfish, Dry fish or meat
  • Red oil

Steps in making Pounded Yam

  1. Peel and wash the yam with water.
  2. Add moderate amount of water to pot and add the yam to it.
  3. Allow to boil for 45-50 minutes until the yam is tender.
  4. Pour the cooked yam into a mortal and start pounding with pestel until it becomes fluffy.
  5. Then knead and serve with any soup of your liking.

Steps in making Egusi soup

  1. Peel, wash and grind the white seed melon.
  2. Mix with water to form a melon paste.
  3. Add oil to pot and let it heat up for a while.
  4. Add the melon paste and leave it for about 30 seconds
  5. Add the seasonings and necessary ingredients of your likings
  6. And cook for about 7 minutes
