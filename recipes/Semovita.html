Semovita and Vegetable Soup

Description

Semovita: White, cooked, paste, soft.

Vegetable Soup: Greenish, chopped, thick.

Ingredients

  • Semovita
  • Red oil
  • Seasonings e.g Curry, Thyme, Maggi etc.
  • Crayfish, Dry fish or meat
  • Fresh chopped vegetables

Steps in making Semovita

  1. Add 2 cups of water to pot and wait till it boils.
  2. Add some potash.
  3. Pour the amount of Semovita to be used into the boiling water.
  4. Stir the mixture together.
  5. Keep stiring until it becomes thick and soft.

Steps in making Vegetable Soup

  1. Pour red oil into pot and leave to heat for some minutes.
  2. Add the seasonings and necessary ingredients of your liking.
  3. Wash the chopped vegetables in warm water.
  4. Add the chooped vegetable to mixture of red oil and seasonings
  5. Stir the mixture uniformly
  6. Cook for about 15-20 mins.
